Kobe Bryant

A legend on and off the court

A timeline of Kobe's amazing feats:

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • 1996 - Drafted to the NBA at 17 by the Lakers. Since Kobe was not of age, his contract had to be cosigned by his parents. He resigned the contract on his own when he turned 18 before the season began.
  • 1997 - Kobe wins his first Slam Dunk Contest
  • 2000-2002 - Kobe, along with his superstar teammate Shaquille O'Neal, win three straight NBA Championships together
  • Mid-2000's - Kobe nicknames himself "Black Mamba"
  • 2005-06, 2006-07 In both these seasons, Kobe leaded the league in scoring.
  • 2006 - January 22, 2006, Kobe scores 81 points against the Toronto Raptors, the second most points scored in a game falling 19 short of Wilt Chamberlain's historic 100.
  • 2009, 2010 - Kobe wins back-to-back championships with the Lakers.
  • 2012-13 - Kobe suffers a torn achilles heel, a career-ending injury
  • 2016 - Kobe officially retires from basketball, scoring 60 points against the Utah Jazz in his final NBA game.
  • 2018 - Kobe wins the Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film for "Dear Basketball".
  • 2020 - Kobe, along with his second oldest daughter, Gianna, and seven others, die in a helicopter crash.
